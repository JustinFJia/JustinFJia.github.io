Music Player

Created a music player interface designed to play an album

Role: Developer (September 2022-December 2022)

Skills: HTML/CSS, Javascript

Process

  1. For the first few parts of the assignment, I just needed to format the album and song pages correctly
  2. I used mostly HTML/CSS to format the two pages
  3. Clicking on the album covers will bring you to the individual album page with the songs
  4. If you want to back out, you can click the back button and it will bring you back
  5. Clicking on an individual song will bring you to the song page

Javascript

  1. For the final part of this project, we had to use Javascript to implement the functionality
  2. We have the play/pause, next song, and previous song button
  3. The plus and minus signs adjust the volume, which is indicated visually by the bars
  4. The timer will increment after each second
  5. When you switch songs, the timer resets
  6. The Song title will also switch
